Dear John,

Thank you for your inquiry about transferring to Rider University. I am happy that you are considering Rider to continue your studies. Our viewbook is on its way to you, but I want to point out a few important points about why Rider is considered one of the most 'transfer friendly' schools in New Jersey.
• We know that transferability of your credits is extremely important to you. We can provide you with an unofficial credit evaluation, even before you apply. Visit our transfer Web site to learn about our generous credit transfer policies.
• Rider partners with community colleges through many Guaranteed Transfer Agreements designed to give you a clear pathway to your future.
• Your strong college performance is rewarded through rich transfer scholarships. Visit our website to see our scholarship criteria.
• It’s easy for transfer students to get involved in clubs and activities as early as their first week at Rider.
• As a transfer student, you can expect the kind of learning that engages your mind and heart. I invite you to become a part of Rider's vibrant living and learning community.

I invite you to visit Rider to get to know us better. Schedule a tour, be a Rider student for a day, or sign up for an open house through our website.
